Kind Habits for Tired Brains

1. The 25-Minute Focus Sprint

Be incredibly kind to yourself: set a timer for just 25 minutes and do what you can. When it rings, immediately walk away, stretch, or get a glass of water for 5 minutes. Your brain deserves that little rest!

2. The "Brain-Dump" Doodle

Don't stress about re-reading heavy paragraphs over and over. Close the book, take a blank page, and gently scribble down or sketch whatever you can remember in your own words. No judgment, just playing with ideas.

3. The Cozy Review Circle

Instead of cramming everything at once, look at your favorite topics tomorrow, then in 3 days, then next week. It takes the pressure off and lets the knowledge settle into your memory like an old friend.
